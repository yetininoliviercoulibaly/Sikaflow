# 🚀 Getting Started Guide - SikaFlow

Welcome to the future of event management! **SikaFlow** transforms your WhatsApp into a true management assistant for your Club, Festival, or Restaurant.

No app to install. No passwords to remember. Everything happens in your chat.

---

## 🏁 1. For Owners

### First Connection

1. Save the SikaFlow number in your contacts: **+1 555 010 9999** (Example).
2. Open WhatsApp and simply send: **"Hi"** 👋.
3. The assistant will guide you to create your first Organization.
   - _Assistant_: "Welcome! I don't see an account. Do you want to create an organization?"
   - _You_: "Yes, create club 'Midnight Blue'"

### Add Your Team

Delegate entry without losing control.
To add a member, send a message in the form:

> "Add +22507070707 as Manager"
> "Add +33612345678 as Staff"

| Role        | What they can do                                                                 |
| :---------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Manager** | Manage operations, see daily reports, report incidents & finances.               |
| **Staff**   | Enter expenses (shopping, ice...), report incidents. No global financial access. |

### Manage Subscription

To activate all features or for a one-off event:

- Send **"Subscription"** or **"Pass"**.
- You will receive a secure payment link (Stripe).
- Once paid, your organization is instantly unlocked.

---

## 💼 2. For Managers & Staff

### Record an Expense 💸

Forget notebooks and lost receipts. Just tell the AI.

- **Option 1 (Text)**: "Bought 10 bags of ice for 5000 FCFA"
- **Option 2 (Voice 🎙️)**: Hold the mic and speak: "I paid the DJ 200 euros in cash right now."
- **Option 3 (Photo 📸)**: Take a photo of the bill. Add caption "Drinks invoice".

> ✅ **SikaFlow** will reply: "Expense recorded: 5000 FCFA (Category: Logistics)".

### Report an Incident 🚨

Safety first. Keep a record of everything happening.

- "Fight at the entrance, security intervened, medium level."
- "Power outage at the VIP bar."

### Request a Report 📊

Need to know where you stand?

- Send: **"Report"** or **"Flash"**.
- You will instantly receive a summary PDF of activity (if you have rights).

## 💡 Tips & Commands

| Command       | Action                                                                        |
| :------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **"Hi"**      | Show main menu.                                                               |
| **"Help"**    | See list of available commands.                                               |
| **"Balance"** | See total expenses/revenue for the session (Managers/Owners).                 |
| **"Switch"**  | If you manage multiple venues, change context (e.g., "Switch Festival Babi"). |
| **"Cancel"**  | Cancel the last action in case of error.                                      |

---

## 🆘 Need Help?

If the assistant doesn't understand, try rephrasing simply or contact support directly by typing **"Human Support"**.
