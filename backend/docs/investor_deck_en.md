# 🦄 Event-Pilot: The Invisible OS for Events Management

**"The power of an ERP, the simplicity of a WhatsApp message."**

---

## 1. The Pain

In the Nightlife and Events industry in Francophone Africa and Quebec, management is **chaotic**.

- **Cash Loss**: 15% to 20% of turnover disappears in "petty cash" expenses that aren't tracked (ice, taxis, staff advances).
- **Unsuitable Tools**: Managers refuse to use complex accounting software (QuickBooks, Sage) on the ground.
- **Shadow Channel**: Everything already happens on WhatsApp (staff groups, photos of invoices), but this data is **unstructured** and unusable.

## 2. The Solution: Event-Pilot

Event-Pilot is not a new app. It is **artificial intelligence layered on top of WhatsApp**.

We don't ask users to change their habits. We transform their natural chat flow into structured data and audible financial reports.

### The Secret Sauce

1.  **Total Multimodality**: Staff send voice notes ("I paid $50 for taxi") or photos of receipts.
2.  **Generative AI (Gemini)**: Our engine understands local jargon (Nouchi, Joual, Slang), extracts context, category, and amount with 99% accuracy.
3.  **Instant Reporting**: The owner receives a P&L (Profit & Loss) PDF every morning, without having entered a single figure themselves.

## 3. The Market

We target two complementary markets:

- **Emerging Markets (Francophone Africa)**:

  - **Size**: +$500M (Informal sector & events).
  - **Need**: Cash traceability & Mobile Money.
  - **Adoption**: WhatsApp is already the internet.

- **Mature Markets (North America / Quebec)**:
  - **Size**: Bar/Restaurant/Festival Industry.
  - **Need**: Tax compliance (GST/QST), tips management, rapid audit.

## 4. Technology & Moats

We have built a **robust and scalable** architecture (NestJS + Microservices) that is hard to replicate quickly.

- **Complex State Machine**: Transparent multi-organization context management.
- **Proprietary OCR & Audio**: AI pipelines fine-tuned on thousands of real data points (club background noise, strong accents).
- **Offline-First**: WhatsApp handles intermittent connectivity for us.

## 5. Business Model

A hybrid model to capture the "Long Tail" of events:

1.  **SaaS Subscription (B2B)**:

    - For permanent establishments (Clubs, Maquis).
    - Recurring Revenue (MRR).
    - Monthly flat fee per organization.

2.  **Event Pass (Pay-as-you-go)**:
    - For festivals, weddings, one-off concerts.
    - One-shot payment for 48h/72h of intensive use.
    - High virality.
